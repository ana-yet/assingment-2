Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten, often referred to as "Kinder," is a crucial stage in a child's early education journey, typically for children aged 3 to 6 years. During this period, children experience significant growth and development in various areas.

Elementary School

Elementary school, typically for children aged 6 to 12 years, marks the beginning of formal education. This period is crucial for laying the academic and social foundations that will support lifelong learning and development.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school, typically for children aged 10 to 16 years, is a transitional period between elementary school and high school. It is a time of significant growth and development, both academically and personally.
